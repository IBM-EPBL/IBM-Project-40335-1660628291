About Project

Problem

 One of the most prevalent and harmful consequences of diabetes mellitus is diabetic retinopathy (DR), which is a major contributor to blindness..DR is an eye disease known to cause moderate to severe visual loss and is the leading cause of blindness in working-age people suffering with long standing diabetes.The health burden is accentuated by the huge per capita cost. This has further increased since the introduction of anti VEGF agents. Very often the disease does not show overt symptoms until it reaches an advanced stage.

Solution

In order to enable the early treatment of diabetic retinopathy in its early stage an image processing algorithm is developed.This newly developed algorithm is intended to atuomate the analysis of fundus images.The new AI approach is instantaneous and cost effective.
